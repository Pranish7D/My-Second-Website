Workshop 1: Introduction to Cybersecurity Fundamentals

About the Workshop:

This workshop provides an overview of cybersecurity fundamentals, covering essential concepts, terminology, and best practices. Participants will gain insights into various threats, attack vectors, and defense mechanisms used in the cybersecurity field.

Requirements:

No prior cybersecurity knowledge is required. Basic computer literacy is recommended.

Certification Key:

Certificate of Completion in "Introduction to Cybersecurity Fundamentals"

Takeaways:

  • Understanding of key cybersecurity concepts
  • Awareness of common cyber threats and vulnerabilities
  • Knowledge about essential security practices
  • Ability to identify potential risks and countermeasures

Target Audience:

  • Individuals new to cybersecurity
  • Students
  • Small business owners
  • Non-technical professionals interested in cybersecurity

Agenda:

  1. Introduction to Cybersecurity
  2. Types of Cyber Threats
  3. Attack Vectors and Techniques
  4. Basic Security Measures
  5. Introduction to Cryptography
  6. Protecting Personal Information Online
  7. Q&A and Discussion
