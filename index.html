    About Amrita School of Engineering

    About Us

    The Amrita School of Engineering organizes workshops focusing on emerging trends in engineering and sciences, catering to school teachers, students, engineering graduates, and faculty members from engineering colleges. These workshops are hosted by various departments/centers within the School of Engineering across different campuses. While some workshops are complimentary, others incur a nominal fee.

    Our institution places high importance on workshops as a vehicle for nurturing hands-on skills, fostering creativity, and encouraging innovation. We are committed to offering a wide array of learning avenues that resonate with the dynamic needs of the industry.
